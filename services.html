FAQ

Common questions about private AI

Do I need an IT department to run a private LLM? +

No. We handle all infrastructure provisioning, model deployment, and ongoing management as part of the retainer. You need a point of contact on your side who can help us understand your workflows — that's it.

Is this actually different from just using ChatGPT? +

Completely. ChatGPT runs on OpenAI's servers, where your queries may be retained, reviewed, or used for training. Our private models run on your infrastructure. Nothing leaves your environment — ever. The model is also trained on your actual data, so it knows your business instead of the internet.

How long does a full Private LLM deployment take? +

6–12 weeks from signed SOW to live system, depending on data volume, integration complexity, and your team's availability for feedback loops. We build in staging checkpoints so you see progress every two weeks.

What data do I need to provide? style="color:var(--teal)">+

For the Audit, just your time and context — no data upload required. For Tier 2/3, we work with whatever is in your existing systems: document libraries, CRM exports, intake forms, case notes, billing records. We sign a DPA before touching anything.

Do you offer ongoing support after deployment? +

Yes — all Tier 3 engagements include a mandatory $3K–$8K/mo retainer that covers ongoing model management, retraining as your data grows, performance monitoring, and workflow updates. Tier 2 clients can add an optional retainer at $2,500–$5,000/mo.
