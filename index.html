The Problem

Public AI tools are a liability your business can't afford.

Every prompt your team types into ChatGPT becomes part of someone else's training data. For regulated industries, that's not a productivity hack — it's an incident waiting to happen.

01 / Exposure

Public AI tools expose your sensitive client data.

The moment a paralegal pastes a contract into ChatGPT, your client confidentiality is theoretically breached. Most firms don't even know it's happening.

02 / Generic

Generic AI gives generic answers — not trained on YOUR business.

Off-the-shelf models don't know your matter types, your pricing, your patient intake forms, or your compliance footprint. So you get advice that sounds smart but isn't yours.

03 / Falling Behind

Your competitors are automating — you're still doing it manually.

Intake, follow-up, document review, client reporting. The firms that built private AI workflows in 2024 are now running on a fraction of the overhead.

The Solution

A private AI stack designed around your business — not the other way around.

We build, deploy and maintain three things that work together: a private LLM, the workflow automation that feeds it, and the dashboards that turn it into decisions.

Private LLM

An open-weights model (Llama, Mistral, or equivalent) deployed on your infrastructure or a private cloud. Your data never leaves your environment — ever.

Workflow Automation

Intake, triage, drafting, follow-up, billing prep — the repetitive operational work that drains your week, automated end-to-end. Most clients reclaim 10+ hours per staff member.

Business Intelligence

Ask your own data plain-English questions and get answers in seconds. Pipeline, retention, capacity, profitability — without exporting a single CSV to a public tool.
