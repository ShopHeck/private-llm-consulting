The Problem

Public AI is a liability your firm can't afford to ignore.

Every time a paralegal pastes a contract into ChatGPT, or a nurse uploads intake notes into Copilot, your sensitive data enters a public model's training pipeline. Most teams don't know it's happening.

01 / Exposure

Your confidential data is training someone else's model.

ChatGPT, Copilot, and Gemini are not built for privileged communications. Under ABA Rule 1.6, uploading client data to these tools may constitute unauthorized disclosure — and most firms have no policy in place.

02 / Generic

Off-the-shelf AI doesn't know your business.

A model trained on the internet knows nothing about your matter types, billing codes, patient intake forms, or compliance requirements. Generic answers from a generic model aren't useful — they're dangerous.

03 / Velocity

Your competitors built private AI last year.

Larger firms have already deployed internal AI that handles intake, document drafting, and billing prep. The boutique market is two years behind — and the gap is widening every quarter.

The Solution

Private AI built around your business. Not the internet.

We deploy open-weights language models (Llama, Mistral) on your own infrastructure, fine-tune them on your data, and wrap them in the workflow automations your team actually uses — all without a single byte leaving your environment.

Private LLM

An open-weights model deployed on your server or VPC. Fine-tuned on your documents, workflows, and terminology. Your team gets AI that sounds like it works for your firm — because it does.

Workflow Automation

Intake, triage, billing narratives, client status updates, document review prep — automated end-to-end. Most clients recover 10–15 hours of staff time per week in the first month.

Business Intelligence

Ask your internal data plain-English questions and get answers in seconds. Pipeline health, retention trends, capacity utilization — without exporting a spreadsheet to a public tool.
