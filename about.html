About

A small firm, deliberately. So clients get a senior operator on every engagement.

Heck Holdings exists to bring the AI capabilities of a Fortune 500 IT department to the boutique professional-services firms that need them most — without the consulting overhead, the rebrand-as-AI pitch deck, or the data-exposure risk.

Founder

Michael Heckert

AI Automation Expert · App Developer

Michael Heckert is an AI automation expert and app developer specializing in workflow optimization and private AI deployments for professional-services businesses. He built Heck Holdings around a single observation: the firms that need AI most — boutique law practices, growing med spas, behavioral health groups, independent RIAs — are the same firms that can't afford to expose client data to public AI tools and can't afford to hire a Fortune 500 IT team to do it properly.

Before Heck Holdings, Michael built and deployed automation systems for e-commerce operators, healthcare practices and service businesses — wiring together CRMs, EHRs, payment systems, communication platforms and custom AI models into operational systems that ran themselves. The pattern repeated across every industry: the highest-leverage automation was almost never the one the vendor was selling. It was the boring, specific, deeply-embedded workflow that nobody outside the business understood well enough to fix.

That pattern is the entire thesis of Heck Holdings. Sit with the operators. Map the actual workflows. Build the private infrastructure. Hand off something that earns its keep on day one and keeps earning for years. Headquartered in Pinellas Park, Florida; serving clients across the U.S.

What we stand for

Three values that decide every engagement, every architecture choice, every line of code.

Security First

Every deployment is private by default. If a workflow can run on infrastructure you control, it does. The day "trust us" became an acceptable answer is the day this firm shouldn't exist.

Measurable ROI

Every engagement starts with a financial model and ends with a measurement of whether we hit it. If a workflow doesn't pay for itself within a year, we don't ship it. No theatre. No "innovation" line items.

White-Glove Delivery

Senior operator on every call. Direct access to the founder for every client. Documentation a junior staffer could follow. We work small on purpose so the standard never slips.
